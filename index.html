I am a PhD Scholar in Economics at the Department of Economic Sciences, IIT Kanpur. My research focuses on Development Economics, Health Economics, and Applied Econometrics, specifically within the Indian context.

My work evaluates how structural changes—like digital expansion and regulatory policies—impact public health, nutrition, and food security.

I hold an M.A. from Jamia Millia Islamia and a B.A. (Hons.) from Ramjas College, University of Delhi.
